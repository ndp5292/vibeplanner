The Process

How It Works

From idea to fully planned event in five simple steps. No experience needed, no vendor contacts required.

1
Tell Us Your Vision

Input Your Event Details

Start by telling VibePlanner what you are envisioning. Choose your event type, set your budget range, guest count, and describe your dream vibe. Minimal chic, garden party, urban luxe, or anything in between.

Example: "Birthday dinner for 20 guests, $300 budget, aesthetic minimalist vibe, rooftop setting."
2
AI Magic

Get Your Personalized Event Plan

Our AI instantly generates a complete, customized event plan. This includes a curated theme with color palette, a detailed layout, a line-by-line budget breakdown, and a full task timeline. All tailored to your specific inputs.

Theme and color palette
Space layout suggestions
Budget line breakdown
Task timeline
3
Live Matching

Match with Verified Local Vendors

Our Live Vendor Matching Engine scans our verified vendor network in real time, filtering by your location, budget, availability, and aesthetic. You will see a curated list of photographers, caterers, decorators, DJs, and more, each with reviews, pricing, and portfolio samples.

Vendors are matched live based on current availability. No outdated listings, no guessing.
4
Content Creator

Generate Your Event Content

Let our AI handle the creative work. Generate a stunning custom invite, on-brand social captions ready to post, and a shareable event poster. All matching your chosen aesthetic, produced in seconds.

Custom Invitations Social Captions Event Posters Save the Dates
5
Final Step

Preview and Host Your Event

Before the big day, use our Event Preview feature to visualize your complete setup. See the layout, color scheme, and full flow come together in a visual mockup. Make tweaks, confirm your vendors, and go host the most memorable event of the year.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is VibePlanner free to use?

+

Yes. VibePlanner has a free tier that gives you full access to event planning and AI-generated content. Vendor matching and premium features are available on our paid plan, designed to be accessible for young adults at every stage of life.

How are vendors verified?

+

Every vendor in our network goes through a manual verification process. We check business credentials, review portfolios, and collect verified user reviews before anyone is listed on our platform.

What if my vibe is hard to describe?

+

That is exactly what our AI is for. You can use a few descriptive words, mood board references, or even a color you love. Our AI is trained to interpret vague aesthetic descriptions and translate them into concrete event plans.

Can I use this outside my local area?

+

Absolutely. VibePlanner works anywhere. The planning tools are location-independent, and our vendor network is expanding to cities across the US. Enter your location and we will show you what is available nearby.
