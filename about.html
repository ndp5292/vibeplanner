Our Story

About VibePlanner

Born from the frustration of planning a milestone event with no vendor connections and no idea where to start. We built the tool we wished we had.

Our Mission

Making Aesthetic Events Accessible for Every Young Host

VibePlanner exists because beautiful, well-organized events should not be reserved for people with industry connections and unlimited budgets. We believe every young adult from 25 to 50 deserves to host something they are proud of, without the overwhelm.

Our AI platform combines smart event planning tools with a real-time vendor marketplace, so you can go from an idea to a fully planned, vendor-matched event in under 30 minutes.
